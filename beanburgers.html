Black Bean Burgers

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked black beans
  • 3/8 cup(s) salsa
  • 1 egg
  • 1.5 teaspoons cidar vinegar
  • 1/2 cup(s) whole wheet flour
  • 1/8 cup(s) cornmeal
  • 3/4 teaspoon(s) salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon(s) pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions

  • Mix beans, salsa, egg, vinegar. Add whole wheat flour, cornmeal, salt and pepper. Mix and stir until well blended. Form into patties.
  • Place oil in a pan on low heat.
  • Place patties in pan and cook until firm and brown on one side. Flip and fry.
  • Serve w/ salsa and sourcream

Recipe from Monty's Blue Plate Diner
